Q:  Does this site use real Bitcoins?

Q:  How long does it take for my bitcoins to show up in my account?

When a player makes a deposit into his/her bitcoin wallet it takes about 15-20 minutes for the funds to show up in the account. Any questions contact the site admin.

Q:  If I create a bet and nobody takes it am I charged for it?

If no one takes a bet you create your account does not get charged. No action on the play. Money comes out of your account when you have action. ie. another player takes it.

Q:  When does the money come out of my account when I make a bet?

A players account will get charged when he or she has action. That means either you take a bet off the public board of bets or another player takes a bet you created.

Q:  How long does it take for the money to show up in my account when I win?

Bet are graded 2-4 hours after they are over with. After the bet is graded winning players are paid. Once the money is in your account you are free to make a withdrawal .

Q:  How much can I bet?

There is no bet limit. Keep in mind someone else has to take your bet.

Q:  How much are the network fees and who pays them?

Each player pays there own network fees. Network fees on are around 0.0002 to 0.0004 btc per bet. That is around .12 cents to .25 cents in US dollars.

Q:  Nobody took my bet, what do I do to get action?

You can always check the public board for bets that you like. If you take a bet off the public board you have action.
